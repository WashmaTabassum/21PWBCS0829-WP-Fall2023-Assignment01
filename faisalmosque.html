Visit to The Faisal Mosque

I was there around 6:00 PM and waited as the sky turned purple. The structure turned orange, and the prayer call echoed throughout the city. It was one of the most spiritual moments. If you can make it to the mosque, make sure you are there at sunset. You will not regret it.
