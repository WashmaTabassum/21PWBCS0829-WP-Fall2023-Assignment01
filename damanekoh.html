Visit to Daman e Koh

At around 3 PM, when the light was just perfect, i along with friends took an Uber up to the Daman-e-Koh viewpoint, where you will be able to see why they call Islamabad the greenest city in the country. There is no better view of the city than this and i would like to go to this viewpoint everytime i want to see the beauty of nature. There were so many little monkies snatching food from people i gave banana to one of them. I had a video that i recorded of monkies playing and snatching food.

I am sharing the video with you guys because i found it so amuzing.
